What is Kidzrunz?

KidzRunz provides fun, positive, community-based activities for young males to not only keep them engaged, entertained, and energised, but to provide important life skills to tackle any behavioural, social and/or domestic difficulties your child may be experiencing. We take a dynamic approach to encouraging children's physical engagement by taking them out into the community and participating in entertaining activities.

Our Packages

You can purchase a certain number of hours which can be used over the space of 3 months. Each booking is inclusive of £20 which will go onto a KidzRunz Activity Voucher used towards the total cost of any activity booked with your child during a session.

E.G. If making a 5-hour booking for the total cost of £175, £20 will go towards a KidzRunz Activity Voucher covering the total cost of a booked activity for your child to enjoy with their Mentor. £155 remaining balance will be used to cover the total cost of the 5 hours booked).
