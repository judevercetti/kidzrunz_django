Director's Story

Meet Kenneth Holder, the Director of KidzRunz. Kenneth's experiences have shaped his passion for caregiving and dedication to supporting and mentoring children in need.

Raised in the care system, Kenneth had a positive experience with his foster carers and maintained a close relationship with them. Despite experiencing the loss of a child, he found strength in his relationship with his daughter and has always been passionate about working with children.

Kenneth is a mentor to his children, nephews, and nieces. He is also a fully qualified Residential Support Worker at a Local Authority Children Care Home in his community. He is committed to providing children with the care, support, and mentorship they need to thrive.

KidzRunz is a passion project for Kenneth, allowing him to do what he loves daily by giving back to needy children. His dedication to making a positive difference in children's lives drives him to provide safe and engaging activities through KidzRunz.

Kenneth's personal story is a testament to his commitment to caring for children, and he is proud to lead the team at KidzRunz in their mission to provide support and mentorship to children in need.

Activities you can do with Kidzrunz

Each parent/carer has the option to top up their KidzRunz Activity voucher to cover any further costs for activities booked for each session. This will be an additional top up to the £20 that is included in the Pricing Package (as explained above).

Please note that money on an Activity Voucher should cover the total costs of any activity planned with your child (please note that this is at your discretion, there are many fun non-monetary activities that your child can enjoy). It is at your discretion how much money you top up on your voucher. Please ensure you top up enough money prior to the start of each session for any planned activities you have booked in with us.

{% comment %}

Money topped up on each voucher can be used up to 1 year after initial top up and can be transferred to an existing or new user of KidzRunz where desired, provided that it is transferred within the 1-year period of which it is valid.

OFFER: KidzRunz will add an additional 10% to any KidzRunz Activity Voucher purchased with a Pricing Package.

(E.G. If purchasing a pricing package and a £200 KidzRunz Activity Voucher in the same transaction, the total money on the Activity Voucher will be £220. KidzRunz will then top up an additional 10% (£22) on the Activity Voucher, making the total amount £242).

Our Packages

You can purchase a certain number of hours which can be used over the space of 3 months. Each booking is inclusive of £20 which will go onto a KidzRunz Activity Voucher used towards the total cost of any activity booked with your child during a session.

E.G. If making a 5-hour booking for the total cost of £175, £20 will go towards a KidzRunz Activity Voucher covering the total cost of a booked activity for your child to enjoy with their Mentor. £155 remaining balance will be used to cover the total cost of the 5 hours booked).
