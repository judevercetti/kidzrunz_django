Director's Story

Meet Kenneth Holder, the Director of KidzRunz. Kenneth's experiences have shaped his passion for caregiving and dedication to supporting and mentoring children in need.

Raised in the care system, Kenneth had a positive experience with his foster carers and maintained a close relationship with them. Despite experiencing the loss of a child, he found strength in his relationship with his daughter and has always been passionate about working with children.

Kenneth is a mentor to his children, nephews, and nieces. He is also a fully qualified Residential Support Worker at a Local Authority Children Care Home in his community. He is committed to providing children with the care, support, and mentorship they need to thrive.

KidzRunz is a passion project for Kenneth, allowing him to do what he loves daily by giving back to needy children. His dedication to making a positive difference in children's lives drives him to provide safe and engaging activities through KidzRunz.

Kenneth's personal story is a testament to his commitment to caring for children, and he is proud to lead the team at KidzRunz in their mission to provide support and mentorship to children in need.
